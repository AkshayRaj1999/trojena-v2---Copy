OWN ONE OF THE FIRST AVAILABLE RESIDENTIAL PROPERTIES IN NEOM

A development like no other on earth, Trojena will redefine luxury mountain living in a place of epic natural beauty, extraordinary architecture and all-season weather.

Situated on Jebel al Lawz, one of the most culturally and geographically significant mountains in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Trojena is located 50km from the Gulf of Aqaba coast in the heart of NEOM’s nature region.

A world-class development, offering a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for early investors and residents who seek unrivalled experiences, outdoor adventure and a vibrant community on the land of the future.

WHAT MAKES TROJENA UNIQUE?

With elevations ranging from 1,500m to 2,500m above sea level, Trojena’s significantly cooler climate makes it possible to provide the Gulf’s first outdoor ski resort.
